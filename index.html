About The Bacchanalia

In Short, It's a Party!


Baccus was the god of wine and revelry, and Ypsilanti is in revival, instead of tents and preaching there'll be burlesque, risque art and spirits to enliven the mood at the Ypsilanti Riverside Arts Center. This is the first annual party to mark the importance of wine and revelry in our lives.


This is event is organized by the Ypsilanti Jaycees which is an organaztion dedicated to training young people to be leaders, we do this by doing stuff, some of it fun, some of it necessary, all of it is a learning experience.
